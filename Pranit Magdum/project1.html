DD Robocon: Lagori Theme

Designed and manufactured two robots, R1 and R2, in accordance with the Lagori theme, and received AIR 3 in the Design phase and overall AIR 10.

R1 was the first robot, and its mission was to strike and break the Lagori Discs, as well as strike the ball on the head.

R2 was the second robot, and its mission was to simultaneously grasp the three balls with the ball gripper and pass these balls to robot R1 as well as grasp the Lagori discs with the disc gripper and pile it in time.
