AUTOMATIC AGRICULTURAL PESTICIDE SPRINKLING ROBOT

Design and Production of an Agricultural Robot for spraying pesticides and other chemicals.

The primary drawbacks during manual spraying are the farmers may come into contact with the pesticide, which could result in skin cancer and asthma attacks. Thus, the use of these types of robots in agriculture reduces the amount of health issues and also time needed to spray the pesticide and helps farmers work in any season or environment.
